Lasagna Recipe

ONE-POT MEALS RECIPE

Enjoy easy meals all season long with these dump-and-go recipes made in one pot, pan or slow-cooker.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup chicken broth
  • 3 Tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chiken breast

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken.
